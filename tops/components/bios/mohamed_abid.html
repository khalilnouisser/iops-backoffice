BIOGRAPHY

Mohamed ABID received his diploma of engineer in computers sciences with distinction from ENSI: National School of Computer Sciences (University of Manouba, Tunisia) in year 2004. He then received Master Degree in Computer Science from ENSI, in year 2005. In February 2011, he successfully completed his PhD in Computer Science and Network, with honorable mention, at University of Pierre and Marie Curie (Paris 6) and Telecom SudParis in France, under the supervision of Prof. Hossam AFIFI.

Currently, Mohamed ABID is an assistant professor in the National Engineering School of Gabes (ENIG). He is a CCNA instructor in the CISCO Academy of ENIG. He is also a member of the research Unit Hatem Bettaher IResCoMath (Informatique, Réseau, Télécommunication et Mathématique UR13ES79). His research interests lie in the area of Network security, privacy, wireless network, NGN, vehicular network, IoT, Big Data, Cloud computing.

Formerly, Mohamed ABID was a member or Cristal Lab in ENSI-Manouba. Then, he was a member of RS2M lab (Networks and mobile multimedia services Laboratory), Telecom SudParis, France.

Mohamed ABID has supervised 2 masters’ students and is co–supervising 2 Phds students in Telecom.

Mohamed ABID is 37 years old. He is married and father to two children.
