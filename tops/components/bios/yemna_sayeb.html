Yemna Sayeb Belhassen

BIOGRAPHY

Yemna Sayeb Belhassen received her Master and PhD degree in Computer Science from The National School of Computer Sciences (ENSI) in the University of Manouba.

She is currently Assistant professor at the Higher Institute of Multimedia Arts of Manouba (ISAMM) and active member in the research group at RIADI Laboratory at National School of Computer Science, ENSI, University of Manouba.
She is certified in Business Intelligence Analyst and Big Data Developer.

2012-2017: Senior Advisor to the President of Manouba University in concern with Information System;
2008- 2011 : Senior Advisor to the Minister of Higher Education, Scientific Research, in concern with Information System and implementing the SALIMA Information;
2007-2011 : Member of the National Commission of University Reform (LMD); 2001-2005 : Head of IT and Multimedia Department in the Higher Institute of Multimedia Arts of Manouba (ISAMM)

Yemna Sayeb is Author or co-author of some publications in scientific conferences, in the field of software engineering, Enterprise Architecture and Urbanization, Business Process Modeling and Governance, Model Driving Architecture.

She supervised master student and co-supervise some doctoral students.
