BIOGRAPHY

Adel Bouhoula received in 1990 a Computer Engineering degree with Distinction from the University of Tunis (Tunisia). He received a Master’s degree, PhD degree with Distinction and Habilitation degree all in computer science from Henri Poincare University in Nancy (France), in 1991, 1994 and 1998, respectively.

Adel Bouhoula is currently a Professor at the Sup’Com Engineering School of Telecommunications in Tunisia and a Visiting Professor at the University of Tsukuba in Japan. He is also the Founder and Head of the «Digital Security» Research Unit, the Founder and President of the Tunisian Society for Digital Security.

Formerly, Adel Bouhoula has served as Chairman and CEO of the Research Institute for Computer Science and Telecommunication in Tunisia (IRSIT). Prior to that, he has been the Director of Information Technologies and Networks at «Tunisie Telecom» and a Senior Researcher with the INRIA French National Institute for Research in Computer Science and Control in France. Also, He has been a Member of the High Level Committee for Science and Technology in Tunisia.

Adel BOUHOULA owned three patents and has published three edited books and over 140 articles in leading international journals, conferences and refereed workshops, since 1992. His research interests include in particular Automated Reasoning, Network Security, Cryptography, Validation of cryptographic protocols, Formal Methods in System Design and Advanced Data Structures and Algorithm Analysis.

Adel BOUHOULA was invited to give seminars/talks, in numerous universities and laboratories all over the world, namely, in France, the United Kingdom, Italy, Portugal, Spain, Germany, Russia, Australia, Canada, China, the United States and Japan. He was also invited as visiting scholar/researcher/professor to several world-known and reputed institutions, to mention a few: SRI International (California - USA), the Mitsubishi Research Institute (Tokyo - Japan), the Laboratory Specification and Verification at the “Ecole Normale Supérieure” of Cachan (Paris – France), the Computer Science Research Laboratory of Bordeaux “LaBRI” (Bordeaux – France), the Hardware Verification Group at Concordia University (Montreal – Canada), the University of Sherbrook (Canada), and the Symbolic Computation Research Group at the University of Tsukuba (Tsukuba – Japan).

Adel BOUHOULA has supervised more than 30 Masters and PhD students in France and Tunisia. He has also supervised many Japanese Masters and PhD students from Tsukuba University, during their internships in Tunisia.

Adel BOUHOULA received the award of the title of the Knight of the Order of the Republic and also the Knight of the National Order of the Merit in the field of the Education and of the Science. He was also awarded by the Carthage university award for Excellence in scientific research as he ranked first in the discipline "telecommunications" among active Tunisian researchers in Tunisia.

Adel BOUHOULA is 50 years old. He is married and father to two children.
